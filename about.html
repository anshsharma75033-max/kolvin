    About Company

    Kolvin Management Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (KMS)

    Kolvin Management Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (KMS) is a professional consulting organization driven by a single mission — to deliver customized, need-based solutions that create measurable impact. Our services are designed to address real-world challenges through a focused, result-oriented approach.

    Our activity spectrum includes consultancy assignments, Integrated Manpower & Security Services, research and studies, skill development initiatives, training and certification programs, placement services, and allied activities. Backed by a young, energetic, and experienced team, we are committed to delivering quality outcomes within stipulated timelines.

    9+

    Years Experience

    Management and development consulting form the strongest pillars of KMS. We actively work across agriculture and allied sectors, forestry and environment, watershed management, socio-economic development, market research, preparation of Techno-Economic Feasibility Reports (TEFRs) and Detailed Project Reports (DPRs), and business process re-engineering.

    Our strength lies in our team of seasoned professionals — both in-house experts and empanelled advisors — who bring deep domain knowledge and practical insights to every assignment.

    KMS is a leading organization engaged across diversified sectors with a strong grassroots focus. Our mission extends beyond business — we strive to enhance employability, productivity, and livelihoods, especially among the weaker sections of society.

    We believe in creating equal opportunities and enabling individuals to achieve social and economic empowerment while remaining firmly focused on livelihood generation and sustainable development.

    Optimal utilization of specialized human resources to meet evolving market needs has been one of our core objectives. Having successfully delivered on this promise in the past, we continue to remain committed to the same vision for the future.
