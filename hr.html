    KMS (Kolvin Management Solutions Pvt. Ltd.) provides reliable and efficient Human Resource Services tailored to meet diverse workforce needs. We specialize in recruitment, staffing, payroll management, and compliance support across multiple industries.

    Our focus is on delivering skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled manpower with quick turnaround and quality assurance. With a strong screening process and industry expertise, we ensure the right talent for the right role.

    At KMS, we simplify HR operations, enabling organizations to focus on their core business while we manage their workforce efficiently.

    RECRUITMENT PROCESS

    Sourcing is done through Internal & External sources like training centers, referrals, transfers, Advertisement etc.

    Initial Screening
    Basic Interview

    Interview process takes place at our recruitment cell which include work experience, height, weight check, trainings.

    Verification done internal as well as through external agency which include education check, criminal check, past work records check, address check, court checks etc.

    Background Check
    Medical Check

    Medical Check is done as per Govt. Guidelines / PSARA. Specific medical check can be done on need basis.
